How to Track Prayer Times on Your Phone

March 24, 2026 · 5 min read

In today's busy world, keeping up with the five daily prayers can be challenging. Between work, school and daily responsibilities, missing the Adhan is easier than it should be. Fortunately, your smartphone can become a reliable spiritual companion.

Why Track Prayer Times on Your Phone?

Traditionally, the call to prayer (Adhan) from the mosque served as the primary reminder. But in modern urban life — inside offices, on public transport or in noisy environments — hearing the Adhan isn't always possible. This is where mobile apps come in:

  • Location-based accuracy: GPS calculates the exact prayer times for your city, automatically.
  • Advance reminders: Get notified 10–30 minutes before each prayer so you have time to prepare.
  • Silent environments: Vibration or silent notifications keep you informed during meetings.
  • Travel-friendly: Prayer times update automatically when you move to a new city or country.

The Benefits of a Consistent Prayer Routine
