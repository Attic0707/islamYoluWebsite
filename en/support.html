Support & Contact

For questions, suggestions or bug reports, reach us through the channels below.

1. Contact

Please send all app-related feedback to the following email address:

Email: islamyoluapp@gmail.com

2. Bug Reports

If you encounter a bug or unexpected behaviour, it helps to include:

  • Your device model (e.g. iPhone 15)
  • Operating system version (e.g. iOS 18.1 or Android 14)
  • App version (shown in Settings)
  • A brief description of how to reproduce the issue

3. Suggestions & Feedback

Feature requests, design ideas or anything you think could improve the app — we welcome all constructive feedback. Don't hesitate to share what's on your mind.

4. Response Time

We do our best to respond within a reasonable time. During busy periods replies may take a little longer — thank you for your patience.
