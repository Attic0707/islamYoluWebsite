Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 12, 2025 — This page explains the privacy practices of the Islam Yolu mobile app.

1. Overview

Islam Yolu is a mobile app that provides prayer times, Adhan reminders, Quranic verses and inspirational content. This privacy policy explains what data is processed when you use the app and how it is protected.

By using the app, you agree to the terms described in this privacy policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Device and usage data

The app may collect limited anonymous usage data for crash detection and performance improvements, such as:

  • App version
  • Device model and operating system version
  • Crash logs

2.2. Location data

To calculate accurate prayer times, we may request access to your approximate location. Location data is:

  • Used only to calculate prayer times and related reminders.
  • Not permanently stored on our servers.

2.3. Notification preferences

Your in-app settings (notifications on/off, sound, vibration, etc.) are stored only on your device and are not linked to your personal identity.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for analytics or advertising (e.g. Google AdMob). These services may process some anonymous data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. How We Use Data

Collected data may be used for:

  • Providing core app functionality (prayer times, notifications, etc.)
  • Crash detection and performance improvements
  • Statistical, anonymous usage analysis
  • Legal compliance when required

5. Data Retention

Most app settings (theme, notification preferences, etc.) are stored only on your device. Any server-side data (if applicable) is retained only as long as necessary for technical and operational purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is designed for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Parents or guardians are responsible for monitoring their children's use of mobile devices.

7. Your Rights

While the app primarily works with anonymous and on-device data, you may always contact us with privacy-related concerns. You may request information about our data processing activities where applicable.

8. Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date on this page. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us via the Support page.
